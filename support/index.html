Support

How can we help?

For Fat2Fit support, billing questions, privacy requests, or app feedback, contact claudejarvis01@gmail.com.

Before You Contact Support

  • Subscriptions: Manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings under your Apple ID subscriptions. Apple handles billing and refunds.
  • Apple Health: Open the Health app or iOS Settings to review which health categories Fat2Fit can read or write.
  • Apple Watch: Make sure your watch is paired, unlocked, nearby, and running the latest available watchOS version.
  • Camera or photos: Confirm Fat2Fit has camera and photo permissions if progress photos, food scanning, or barcode scanning are not working.
  • Notifications: Check iOS notification settings if reminders or streak alerts are not appearing.

What to Include

When possible, include your iPhone model, iOS version, Fat2Fit app version, a short description of the issue, and screenshots or screen recordings that show the problem.

Privacy and Data Requests

For privacy questions or data deletion help, email claudejarvis01@gmail.com. Please do not include sensitive medical information in your first support email.
